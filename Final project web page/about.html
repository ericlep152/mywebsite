My Family

I was born into a French/Italian/American Home, My father being an Italian raised in France and my mother living accross the United States. I have 3 Siblings, all younger than me. I grew up in America and France for about an equal amount of time in both.

Friends

Friends are very important to me. I was blessed to have a very tight group of friends all throughout High school and to today. I would do anything for them. They are my second family.

My Happy Place

Almost every year since I was a kid, my family goes to Lake Powell for a week or two. I love that place so much. It's a place where I can disconnect from the busy world and spend quality time with friends and family.

Travel

Traveling is one of my favorite hobbies. I have been lucky enough to visit: France, Spain, Italy, Austria, Switzerland, Germany, Belgium, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Poland and 4 Islands in French Polynesia.
